Story

For as long as I can remember I have been interested in the efficiency, the effectiveness, and the ingenuity which can be created through the use of modern technologies. I believe that through cooperation and coordination, small teams of developers can change the world, one line of code at a time.


After graduating from Northern Michigan University with highest honors in Law and Economics I gravitated toward a passion I have held for so long, but had never thought I would pursue in a professional capacity, namely software development.
